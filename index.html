The Hidden Infrastructure Tax

You are paying to host CSS and JS for machines that don't have eyes.

When OpenAI, Perplexity, or a hundred different travel-aggregation crawlers hit your site, they trigger the exact same database queries, React rendering, and massive HTML payloads as a human.

They download your tracking pixels, your cookie banners, your massive CSS bundles, and your heavy navigation megamenus. They immediately throw away 80% of that data to find the text hidden inside. But AWS still charges you for 100% of the outbound transfer.

AXO intercepts these bots. Instead of letting them hit your origin for 16,000 bytes of HTML, we serve them a raw 3,000-byte Markdown file heavily cached at the edge. Your compute costs drop instantly.

Three Lines of Config

AXO sits between your load balancer and origin. No code changes required.

1

Identify Scrapers

AXO precisely identifies aggressive AI agent requests by user-agent, headers, and rapid request behaviors. Human visitors pass straight through.

2

Shed the Bloat

Your heavy visual HTML is instantly converted into lightweight Markdown. We strip out all CSS, JS, and layout structure that machines don't need.

3

Protect the Origin

The tiny Markdown payloads are cached aggressively at the edge. The scrapers get the data they want, and your origin server stays completely silent.
